// Sponsor FAQ

Questions From Sponsors

What is Breakthrough OKC?+
Breakthrough OKC is a full-day real estate investing summit (September 26, 2026) bringing together serious investors, vendors, and speakers at the Champion Convention Center in Oklahoma City.
What do sponsors & exhibitors get?+
Direct exposure to 250+ serious real estate investors, access to attendee contact lists, on-stage visibility at higher tiers, logo placement on printed materials and the website, raffle integration for lead capture, and event tickets for your team.
Who should consider sponsoring or exhibiting?+
Real estate service providers (lenders, contractors, property managers), companies offering investment products or financing, businesses seeking direct ROI through leads and networking, and firms wanting maximum exposure through live presentations.
What is the raffle?+
The raffle is a high-engagement lead-generation opportunity available to all exhibitors and sponsors. You provide a prize in exchange for attendee contact information — increasing booth traffic and building rapport.
Do I have to run a raffle?+
No — it's optional. But most participants choose to, because it significantly increases booth traffic and lead collection.
