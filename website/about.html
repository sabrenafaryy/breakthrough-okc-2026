// About The Summit

Why Breakthrough OKC Exists

The biggest wealth-building window in a generation is opening in Oklahoma City. Most investors will watch it pass. This summit is for the ones who won't.

Oklahoma City is moving billions of public dollars into redevelopment — TIF, a new $1B arena, MAPS 4, and housing funds all at once. When a city floods an area with capital, values rise. The investors positioned early win. The rest buy in after.

// Our Mission

Turn A Once-In-A-Generation Boom Into Real, Repeatable Wealth

Breakthrough OKC is a one-day, in-person summit built to do one thing: give investors the frameworks, the access, and the plan to capitalize on OKC's redevelopment cycle — and to run those same plays in any market they operate in.

It's not a pitch-fest and it's not theory. It's proven operators teaching how they actually do it, the city leaders directing the money in the room to answer questions directly, and a working environment where you build your own plan and meet the people who can fund it.

Who it's for

Serious real estate investors and operators — whether you're doing your first deal or your fiftieth. The frameworks scale with you. If you've got capital (or access to it) and you're ready to move, this room was built for you. And if you're newer but serious, you'll leave with a map most people take years to find.

What makes it different

Most events sell you information you could Google. Breakthrough sells you what a PDF can't: access to the people directing OKC's public capital, application of the frameworks to your own deals, and the room — investors, operators, lenders, and contractors in one place, ready to help you get a deal done.
